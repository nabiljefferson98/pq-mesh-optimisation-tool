    (planarity, fairness, closeness) and records how each one
    affects the final mesh quality.

    weight combinations where you cannot improve one objective
    (e.g. flatness) without making another worse
    (e.g. smoothness or shape change).

    Results are saved to data/output/weight_sensitivity/.
